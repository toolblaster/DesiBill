How to Write a Cheque Correctly?

Writing a cheque requires precision to avoid rejection. Follow these standard RBI guidelines:

1. Amount in Words

Write the exact amount in words. Do not leave large spaces between words. Always end the sentence with "Only" (e.g., "Five Thousand Only") to prevent anyone from adding more words.

2. Amount in Figures

Write the number in the box provided. End it with "/-" (e.g., "5000/-"). Ensure the written words match this number exactly. Discrepancies lead to dishonored cheques.
