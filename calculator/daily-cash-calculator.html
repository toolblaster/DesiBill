Frequently Asked Questions

Does this save my data online?

No. This is a 100% client-side tool. Your cash details never leave your browser, ensuring complete privacy of your financial data.

How to use the WhatsApp feature?

After entering your counts, click "Copy for WhatsApp". The tool formats the list (e.g., "500 x 5 = 2500") so you can paste it directly into a chat.

What should I do if my physical cash does not match the sales report?

If there is a mismatch (cash shortage or excess), re-count the denominations using this tool. Check for unrecorded expenses (petty cash) or billing errors before closing the day.

Is this tool suitable for high-volume businesses like Petrol Pumps?

Absolutely. This tool is optimized for high-speed entry and supports all Indian denominations, making it perfect for cash-heavy businesses like petrol pumps, retail stores, and restaurants.
