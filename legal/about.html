Our Mission

Empowering Bharat's Vyaparis with Simple Digital Tools

DesiBill is built on a simple promise: Software should be free, fast, and private. We help shopkeepers, freelancers, and small business owners manage their daily finances without complex ERPs or monthly subscriptions.

100% Privacy Focused

We don't have a backend database. Your client lists, invoices, and cash counts are stored only in your browser (Local Storage). We cannot see or sell your data.

No Login Required

Business happens fast. You shouldn't have to remember passwords or wait for OTPs just to print a bill. Open the site and start working instantly.

Always Free

Essential tools like Invoice Generators and GST Calculators should be utilities, not products. DesiBill is and will remain free for basic business use.
