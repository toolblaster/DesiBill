Your Data is Yours

Privacy Policy

DesiBill is built with a "Privacy First" architecture. We do not have servers to store your invoices or customer data.

1. No Data Collection

DesiBill functions entirely in your web browser (Client-Side). When you create an invoice, calculate GST, or count cash, none of that information is sent to us. We do not have a backend database to store your business details.

2. Local Storage

To improve your experience (e.g., remembering your shop name, logo, or saved clients), we use your browser's Local Storage technology. This data lives on your specific device and is never uploaded to the internet.

You can clear this data at any time by clearing your browser cache or using the "Reset" buttons within our tools.

3. Third-Party Services

We use standard Content Delivery Networks (CDNs) to load resources like fonts and icons:

  • Tailwind CSS & Font Awesome: For styling and icons.
  • Google Fonts: For typography.

These services may collect basic technical data (like IP address) to deliver the files, governed by their respective privacy policies.

4. Cookies

DesiBill does not use tracking cookies for marketing or analytics. We believe in providing a clean, ad-free utility for small businesses.

5. Changes to Policy

Although highly unlikely given our architecture, if we change how we handle data, we will update this page immediately.

6. Contact
